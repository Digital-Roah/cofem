Welcome to Cofem Worldwide

Chariots of Fire Worldwide Evangelical Outreach Ministries (COFEM) is an evangelical outreach ministry with the sole aim of bringing believers closer to christ through preaching, teaching and evangelizing the word of God. We organise impactful Healing, Teaching, Prophetic and Deliverance programmes online which has helped alot of believers and unbelievers in building their faith and relationship with God as followers of Christ.

Through all these meetings alot of testimonies has surfaced and is completely evident by the way the power of God moves in our services and the impactations of the holy spirit on the lives of people fellowshipping with COFEM.
